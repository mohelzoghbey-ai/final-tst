About TST

Building a space for creative and tech-passionate individuals

Who We Are

TST (Technology Society Team) is an independent youth tech community that aims to build a space for creative and tech-passionate individuals.

We are the first startup company responsible for organizing events in the Delta region. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech innovators through workshops, events, and collaborative learning experiences.
